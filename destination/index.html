TOURIST ATTRACTIONS

WANA WISATA SUMBER BIRU

Wana Wisata Sumber Biru presents a tourist experience while culinary in the middle of the river. Sumber Biru is located in Wonotirto Hamlet, Wonomerto Village, Wonosalam District, Jombang Regency.The uniqueness of this place lies in the placement of dining chairs in the middle of the river. Visitors can also capture cool moments because there are various interesting photo spots there.

TRETES WATERFALL

Tretes Wonosalam Waterfall is still in the same area in Wonosalam Sub-district. Precisely in the Raden Soerjo Great Forest area, Teaching Hamlet, Galengdowo Village. With a height of 158 meters, Wonosalam Tretes Waterfall is claimed to be among the highest waterfalls in East Java.

PINE HILL

This tour is located in a hilly area with pine tree forest vegetation. Bukit Pinus Tourism is located in Carangwulung Village, Wonosalam District, Jombang Regency. In addition to treats of natural beauty, Bukit Pinus Wonosalam also provides exciting rides and interesting photo spots. One of the rides that can be tried, namely the hanging bicycle ride.

KEDUNG CINET

Located in Klitih Village, Plandaan District, Kedung Cinet is an exotic tourist attraction in Jombang. In fact, this tour is predicted to be a miniature of the Grand Canyon of the United States. Its exoticism can be seen in the distinctive carvings on the limestone rocks. The rocks appear to surround Kedung Cinet with a slope angle reaching 45 to 90 degrees.
