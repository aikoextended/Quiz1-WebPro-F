JOMBANG DISTRICT

Jombang is a regency located in the central part of East Java Province, Indonesia. Jombang is known as the “City of Santri” due to the large number of Islamic educational institutions (pondok pesantren) in the area. There is even a saying that Jombang is the center of Islamic boarding schools in Java because almost all founders of Islamic boarding schools in Java must have studied in Jombang. .


Famous Indonesian figures who were born in Jombang Regency are the 4th President of the Republic of Indonesia: K.H. Abdurrahman Wahid (Gus Dur), National Hero: K.H. Hasyim Asy'ari and K.H. Wahid Hasyim, Islamic Intellectuals: Nurcholis Madjid, and Culturist: Emha Ainun Najib
